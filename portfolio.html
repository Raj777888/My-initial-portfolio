About Me

Hello! I'm Raj Bhusal, a learner and aspiring creative currently diving into the world of digital design. I’m passionate about turning ideas into visuals, especially through Photoshop, which I’m studying as part of my computer class.

I believe that great design tells a story, and I'm focused on building those stories — one edit, one layout at a time. I often get inspired by my friend Roney, a Photoshop expert who handles his work with precision and professionalism.

Outside of learning, I value deep connections, honesty, and expressing myself both visually and emotionally. Whether it's through design or words, I always try to bring something real to the table.
